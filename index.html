Hi. We are Group-57.

And this is our project, Analyzing the proliferation of fake news: "The Marcos family cleared ALL allegations that were filed against the Marcos family.", with the goal of identifying certain characteristics of users involved in conversations relating to the topic.

Here's an overview of our project.

Research Question

Is there a correlation between the tweets claiming that the Marcos family won all cases filed against the Marcos family, and the negative portrayal of the Aquinos or the Liberal Party?

Hypothesis

Tweets claiming that the Marcoses won all cases are likely to also portray the Aquinos or the Liberal Party negatively.

Null Hpothesis

TShere is no correlation between the tweets claiming that the Marcos family won all cases filed against the Marcos family, and the negative portrayal of the Aquinos or the Liberal Party

Action Plan: Analyze the users' tweets to see if they attack the Aquinos or Liberal Party, or portray them as malicious or incompetent.
